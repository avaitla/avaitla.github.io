Welcome Hello There! Welcome to a University of Chicago Architecture Tour. We have a lot of cool stuff on campus, so for the unfamiliar this is a great introduction to the rich architecture of the University. There are three parts, and you will need to go back to this page to choose each one. It's best to follow along by walking it in the real world, but if you aren't from the University you can still take the tour thanks to the power of technology! Just use Google's awesome street view to move through the map! You can hover over markers on the map or on the right hand pane to get more information about the exhibit indicated. In order to see high quality pictures simply click on the marker or the item in the right hand pane. If you want specific directions on how to take the route, simply click on the 'Directions' button in the right hand pane! In addition, a campus map can be found here. Happy Travels!
